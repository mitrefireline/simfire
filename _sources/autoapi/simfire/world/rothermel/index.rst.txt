.. py:function:: compute_rate_of_spread(loc_x, loc_y, new_loc_x, new_loc_y, w_0, delta, M_x, sigma, h, S_T, S_e, p_p, M_f, U, U_dir, slope_mag, slope_dir)

   Compute the basic Rothermel rate of spread. All measurements are assumed to be in
   feet, minutes, and pounds, and BTU.

   :param loc_x: The current x location
   :param loc_y: The current y location
   :param loc_z: The current z elevation
   :param loc_x: The new x location
   :param loc_y: The new y location
   :param loc_z: The new z elevation
   :param w_0: The oven-dry fuel load of the fuel at the new location
   :param delta: The fuel bed depth of the fuel at the new location
   :param M_x: The dead fuel moisture of extinction of the fuel at the new location
   :param sigma: The Surface-area-to-volume ratio of the fuel at the new location
   :param h: The fuel particle low heat content
   :param S_T: The fuel particle total mineral content
   :param S_e: The fuel particle effective mineral content
   :param p_p: The fuel particle oven-dry particle density
   :param M_f: The envrionment fuel moisture
   :param U: The envrionment wind speed
   :param U_dir: The envrionment wind direction (degrees clockwise from North)
   :param slope_dir: The angle of the steepest ascent at the location

   :returns: The computed rate of spread in ft/min
   :rtype: R
