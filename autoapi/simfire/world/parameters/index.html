class simfire.world.parameters.Environment#

These parameters relate to the environment of the tile. For now we’ll assume these values are constant over a small area. The wind speed and direction can be a constant value, nested sequences, or numpy arrays. The FireManager will convert the constant values and nested sequences to numpy arrays internally.

Parameters

  • M_f – Fuel moisture (amount of water in fuel/vegetation). 1-3% for SoCal, usually never more than 8% for SoCal.

  • U – Wind speed at midflame height (ft/min).

  • U_dir – Wind direction at midflame height (degrees). 0 is North, 90 is East, 180 is South, 270 is West.

M_f :float#
