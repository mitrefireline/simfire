simfire.world.rothermel.compute_rate_of_spread(loc_x, loc_y, new_loc_x, new_loc_y, w_0, delta, M_x, sigma, h, S_T, S_e, p_p, M_f, U, U_dir, slope_mag, slope_dir)#

Compute the basic Rothermel rate of spread. All measurements are assumed to be in feet, minutes, and pounds, and BTU.

Parameters:

  • loc_x (numpy.ndarray) – The current x location

  • loc_y (numpy.ndarray) – The current y location

  • loc_z – The current z elevation

  • loc_x – The new x location

  • loc_y – The new y location

  • loc_z – The new z elevation

  • w_0 (numpy.ndarray) – The oven-dry fuel load of the fuel at the new location

  • delta (numpy.ndarray) – The fuel bed depth of the fuel at the new location

  • M_x (numpy.ndarray) – The dead fuel moisture of extinction of the fuel at the new location

  • sigma (numpy.ndarray) – The Surface-area-to-volume ratio of the fuel at the new location

  • h (numpy.ndarray) – The fuel particle low heat content

  • S_T (numpy.ndarray) – The fuel particle total mineral content

  • S_e (numpy.ndarray) – The fuel particle effective mineral content

  • p_p (numpy.ndarray) – The fuel particle oven-dry particle density

  • M_f (numpy.ndarray) – The envrionment fuel moisture

  • U (numpy.ndarray) – The envrionment wind speed

  • U_dir (numpy.ndarray) – The envrionment wind direction (degrees clockwise from North)

  • slope_dir (numpy.ndarray) – The angle of the steepest ascent at the location

  • new_loc_x (numpy.ndarray) –

  • new_loc_y (numpy.ndarray) –

  • slope_mag (numpy.ndarray) –

Returns:

The computed rate of spread in ft/min

Return type:

R
